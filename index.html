What We Do

Besides just being awesome, the technology committee manages all of APSA's technology infrastructure. Some of the tasks are as simple as email account management while others consist of hosting and maintaining large web applications such as the Residency Wiki Database. The main areas of focus include managing the main webdsite, the annual meeting website, and several physician-scientist-focused databases. Many of these responsibilities involve significant interaction with other committees, including the Public Relations Committee and the Policy Committee.

Technology Committee Chair
